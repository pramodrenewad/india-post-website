About Us

India's Most Trusted
Postal Network

India Post is the government postal system of India, operating under the Ministry of Communications. It is the most widely distributed postal system in the world.

📮
170+ YEARS OF TRUST
Our Story

From Colonial Mails to
Digital India

Established in 1854, India Post has evolved from carrying colonial letters to powering digital payments, insurance, and e-commerce logistics across India.

🏛️

Government Backed

Operated by the Ministry of Communications, ensuring reliability and safety for all transactions.

📦

Widest Reach

Serving every village and city across India — over 1.5 lakh post offices nationwide.

💰

Financial Inclusion

Offering savings accounts, insurance, and pension schemes to rural and urban India alike.

What We Offer

Our Services

From sending a letter to managing your savings — India Post offers a comprehensive suite of services for every Indian.

✉️

Mail Services

Send letters, postcards, and registered mail across India and internationally. Reliable delivery for personal and business correspondence.

Domestic & International
📦

Parcel & Speed Post

Fast, tracked delivery of parcels and packages. Speed Post guarantees delivery within 1–3 business days to major cities.

Express Delivery

Post Office Savings Bank

Open savings accounts, recurring deposits, and fixed deposits with attractive interest rates and government guarantees.

POSB · FD · RD

Postal Life Insurance

Affordable life insurance and Rural Postal Life Insurance plans with low premiums and guaranteed returns.

PLI · RPLI
